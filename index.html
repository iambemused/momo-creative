The approach

From the
centre outward

Most tools for recording a life impose a structure from outside — categories, templates, scoring systems. These tools start from the inside. You are the centre. Your layers, your relationships, your world events are defined by you.

Privacy here is not a policy or a promise. It is an architecture. Private events are never included in any export file — they are not present in the data at all. There is nothing to intercept. There is nothing to breach.

The work of understanding your own life is worth doing. These tools try to make it a little easier.

How they work
01
Local & private by default

Both apps run in your browser, store data on your device, and require no internet connection. Nothing is transmitted anywhere.

02
No account required

Open the file in a browser. That's it. No signup, no login, no subscription, no email address.

03
Privacy is structural

Private events are excluded from all exports at the architecture level — not by policy, by mechanism. The data is simply not there.

04
Sharing is deliberate

Nothing crosses into The Crossroads automatically. Every person brings their own Shared Self, consciously, on their own terms.

05
Withdrawal is unconditional

Anyone can update or withdraw their presence from The Crossroads at any time, for any reason, without explanation.

06
Free, always

Both tools are and will remain free for personal use. Support is welcome but never expected. No features are locked.
